F.A.Q.

Q: What is an agent?
A: An agent is someone that acts on behalf of another. We will be there to help you with the business side, and we can even help you find gigs.

Q: Are guys are recruiters?
A: Nope, a recruiter is an individual hired and employed by a company to fill open positions. We are hired and emloyed by you, the freelancer.

Q: Oh, so you are a consulting firm?
A: Nope, you don't work for us. We work for you.

Q: Do I need an agent?
A: Do you like to handle the business side of things? If you do then maybe you don't need an agent. There are a lot of freelancers that enjoy all aspects of the freelancing life. If you enjoy the freelacing life, but don't care for the business side of things then we might be right for you.
